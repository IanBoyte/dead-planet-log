I have conferred with the crew, and we have decided to travel south to the tower and the crash site of The Defiance.

We encountered a member of The Defiance crew, PFC Hudson, under attack by a swarm of flying, worm-like creatures. We were able to rescue him, and he has accepted to join the crew of the Matron's Call.

We have found a supply cache, with a supplement of medical supplies. These will be crucial to our survival in our remaining time on this planet.

We have made our way to the crash site, and have completed an initial external survey of The Defiance.

**DEFIANCE ANALYSIS:**

- Salvagable, but requires major repairs
- Holds a large supplement of rations and supplies, according to PFC Hudson
- Promising to investigate, but likely trapped by former survivors of the crash
